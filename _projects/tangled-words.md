# Tangled Words

Letters in the word have tangled up, choose the right word from the four options.

Simple scramble-like game.

Languages:
- English
- French
- German
- Portuguese
- Russian
- Spanish

Controls:
- Mouse
- 1, 2, 3, 4 hotkeys to select the word
